NoKeyAuth — Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

NoKeyAuth is designed to operate without collecting, storing, or transmitting personal data to our servers.

All cryptographic operations are performed locally on your device. Your authentication secrets are split into shares distributed across your physical devices (iPhone and NFC cards or USB-C drives). No share or secret is transmitted to any server controlled by us.

NoKeyAuth does not include analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

System-level services provided by Apple (such as crash reports or diagnostics) may operate independently of NoKeyAuth.

Contact: privacy@nokeyauth.com
